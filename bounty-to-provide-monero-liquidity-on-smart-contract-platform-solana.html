Bounty to provide Monero liquidity on smart contract platform (Solana)

3 days ago normie-investooooooor
Description

To increase awareness of Monero to new investors it is important to have Monero available as a purchasing option on smart contract enabled AMM based exchanges. Although the privacy offered by the Monero blockchain is of utmost importance, exposure to the Monero asset by retail investors is also important.

There are a few trust minimized projects that currently are involved with tokenizing other blockchains onto smart contract platforms, including Keep Project and Ren project.
No well established projects have been found that are working towards a trust minimized tokenization of Monero.
There are application specific blockchains such as Thorchain and Secret Network that have tokenized Monero, but those are mainly there to provide exit liquidity for those respective chains. So for the sake of this bounty, they will be ignored.

This leaves wXMR currently on Ethereum.

https://blog.btse.com/introducing-wrapped-monero/

https://coinmarketcap.com/currencies/wrapped-monero/

The Monero reservers for wXMR are held by the custodian BTSE. A reserver proof has been publicized.

https://support.btse.com/en/support/solutions/articles/43000610660-wrapped-monero-wxmr-proof-of-reserves

This bounties purpose is to bring wXMR liquidity to Solana. Retail investors cannot afford the costs of transacting on Ethereum, so many have ended up within the Solana ecosystem.

The bounty will be executed in the following steps:

  1. Purchase ETH + wXMR (via BTSE Exchange or uniswap v2)
  2. Bridge wXMR to Solana (https://wormholebridge.com/)
  3. Purchase SOL + xxx (the base pair, could be USDC, SOL, wBTC, etc) (most exchanges)
  4. Create a permissionless pool on Raydium (https://raydium.gitbook.io/raydium/permissionless/creating-a-pool)

Associated costs:
ETH gas uniswap ~$150
Source - https://etherscan.io/gasTracker
Bridging costs ~$100
Source - guessing
Listing on Serum ~$800
Source - https://docs.projectserum.com/serum-ecosystem/building-on-our-vision/add-a-market-on-serum-serum-academy
IL (Impermanent Loss) - depends on the popularity of the pool and the base pair that is chosen (no way to estimate this)

Total Costs: ~$950 + IL ($0 - ~$1,000)

Required Up-front capital for the two-sides of the liquidity pool:
~$5,000 equivalent XMR & ~$5,000 equivalent xxx (the base pair, could be USDC, SOL, wBTC, etc)
Source - https://raydium.gitbook.io/raydium/permissionless/creating-a-pool - "However if total liquidity in the LP is below the equivalent of $10,000 USD at any given time, the AMM will pause placing orders until additional liquidity is added or token prices rise to meet the threshold."

Payout requirements

  • A Serum Market ID is posted here for the xxx/wXMR pair
  • Pull request create for https://github.com/raydium-io/raydium-ui to add the USDC/wXMR pair (the pull request doesn't have to be accepted)
  • Provide liquidity for 2 months after pool inception
